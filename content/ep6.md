Life moves on.

I decide, I can’t keep being pathetic.

We never talk. We actively ignore each other.

So I do what any sensible college student would do.

I try to move on.

I date someone else — partly because I wanted to experience it at least once, 

and partly because I needed proof that I was capable of moving forward.

Things are… fine.

And yet — inconveniently —

She continued living rent-free in the back of my head.

I realised this during one of my mistaken adventures of alcohol and bad decisions again.

Drunk. Overconfident. Again.

I admit it out loud.

In front of my then girlfriend.

Yep! That happened.

God knows why she stayed.

I certainly don’t.

And now P unknowingly knows why she sometimes caught looks from someone across the room.

My bad I say.
