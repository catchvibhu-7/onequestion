This is a story that V and P have shared for a little over nine years, told entirely from V’s side. It contains memories, mistakes, missed chances, and jokes that somehow survived all of that. For the sake of the story, V will occasionally be referred to as “I”. This is intentional. Also lazy. I hope you enjoy this. And yes — the bad puns stay.
